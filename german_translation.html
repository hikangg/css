German Translation

There are over 120 million native speakers of German, primarily in Europe though German is also spoken regionally by native speaker minorities in about 40 countries. It is estimated that German is spoken as a foreign language by an additional 80 million people worldwide. Historically, the German language originated from first contact with the Romans. Latin influence accounts for German as an inflected language with declinations similar to Latin.

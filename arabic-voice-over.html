Arabic Voice-over

When a corporation or a client decides to translate their commercial, presentation in Arabic, Alpha Translation Svcs, Inc. has the knowledge and technical capability to generate Arabic voice-over. Whether the targeted translation was Egyptian Arabic translation, Saudi Arabic translation, Moroccan Arabic translation, etc... we have a variety of Arabic narrators.

With over 10 years' experience of multilingual voice-overs, Alpha Translation Svcs, Inc. has created an extensive library of Arabic and native voice actors, commentators and narrators. In simple words, the Arabic voice-over consists in taking an English audiovisual media and turning it into a localized Arabic language once we complete the Arabic translation of the original voice/text. How do we do it? The Arabic translation length must be timed properly and must match the English source text. Arabic translated text cannot be longer or shorter than the source language. We have the technical capability to provide studio recording added to a team of expert Arabic narrators with extraordinary voice talents.

The fundamental part of our Arabic voice-over production is our Arabic talents. They have the voice flexibility which enables them to perform a plethora of different Arabic characters of varying ages: from the dramatic to the animated, from children to the elderly. Moreover, they have done extensive vocal work for Arabic promotional and instructional video projects, corporate presentations, and documentaries. Whether our clients need a sophisticated, elegant, warm, natural, professional, charming, sensual, conversational, real, corporate Arabic voice, Alpha Translation Svcs, Inc. has the resources to deliver the Arabic voice-over within budget and on-time. Whether you need a talented Lebanese narrator, Jordanian, Iraqi, Syrian, Kuwaiti or Sudanese, Alpha Translation Svcs, Inc. is your answer. Please contact us for all your Arabic voice-over needs.

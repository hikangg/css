Polish Translation

Every Polish translation serviced by Alpha Translation Svcs, Inc. is intended for individuals in Poland where Polish is the official language. Professional Polish translation is also provided to companies with operation in Poland or organizations serving the Polish diaspora.

Polish, a West Slavic language, is the official language of Poland. In written form, the standard Polish alphabet is derived from the Latin alphabet and is currently the largest spoken language of the West Slavic group. The Polish language became far more standardized in the second half of the 20th century. Even though differences between the dialects seem minor, the populations of different regions still speak general Polish somewhat differently. The most significant of these variations are Greater Polish, Lesser Polish, Masovian and Silesian.

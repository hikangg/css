Dutch Translation

Dutch translation is designed for people from the Netherlands, Aruba, Curacao and companies doing businesses in any of these countries. Dutch, the official language of the Netherlands, is called "Flemish" in Flanders, Belgium. Dutch is used in all official and administrative matters and has a close orthographic and syntactic resemblance with German; however, French and especially English have influenced Dutch technical terms.

Dutch Translation

Dutch is also the official language of Suriname and Curacao but Creole is rapidly replacing Dutch in these countries. There are other languages that are linguistically affiliated with Dutch such as Afrikaans and Friesian.

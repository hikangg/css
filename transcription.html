Transcription

Alpha Translation Svcs, Inc. offers general transcription services to clients from across the United States. Since 2001, we have developed the expertise which enables us to understand our clients' requirements and to deliver professional transcription with translation from any language to English. Additionally, our services include transcribing services from any language. We are capable of converting foreign audio to its native language or to English text. Last but not least, we can convert voice to text.

Audio transcription can be briefly described as a general term to refer primarily to a variety of media such as conference recordings, digital audio transcription, interviews and meetings among other things. Alpha Translation Svcs, Inc. has the technical capability and human resources to cover the full range media that include audio transcription.

The first step consists in receiving the audio sound files and video formats in mp3, mpg, dss, wav, wma, as CD or DVD sent by mail and/or through FTP, emails. We prepare a translation quote which includes transcription. Clients can also get their transcriptions from regular audio tapes, mini tapes and micro cassettes.

Alpha Translation Svcs, Inc. has a dedicated team of professional transcribers and translators working tirelessly to deliver accurate transcriptions and translations. Our goal is to provide multimedia companies top-level accurate transcripts and translations of foreign movies, videos or audio recordings.

When dealing with foreign one-on-one interviews, we can render either a clean verbatim transcription and translation meaning that the interviewee's responses are verbatim and that all double words, non-verbal utterances (uh, hum, stutters) are left out. We can also provide a true verbatim transcription and translation which will include all non-verbal utterances of the interviewee.
